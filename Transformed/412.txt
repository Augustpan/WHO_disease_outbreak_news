
2000 - Rift Valley fever in Saudi Arabia - Update 3

25 October 2000 Disease Outbreak Reported

As of 23 October 2000, 443 cases with 85 deaths of
Rift Valley fever were reported by the Ministry of Health of Saudi Arabia. However, there
appears to be a declining trend in the rate of occurrence of cases and deaths.Â  All
cases occurred in Jizan and Asir provinces and the disease has not spread to the
north-west area of the country.

A joint technical team has been formed by the Ministries of
Health and Agriculture of Saudi Arabia and Yemen to control the outbreak.
