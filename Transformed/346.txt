
1999 - Influenza A(H9N2) in Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of China (SAR)

07 April 1999 Disease Outbreak Reported

Influenza A(H9N2) viruses have been identified in two hospitalised children, ages 1 and
4 years, in Hong KongÂ  SAR. One of the children is from Kowloon, and the other from
  Hong Kong Island. Analysis of specimens sent to the WHO Collaborating Centre for Influenza
  in London, UK confirmed the viruses as A(H9N2) subtype which appear similar antigenically
  to swine viruses isolated in Hong Kong SAR in 1998. The children were sent home apparently
  without any medical complications. Further analysis of the viruses is being carried out at
  WHO Collaborating Centres in Atlanta, USA and London, UK.
