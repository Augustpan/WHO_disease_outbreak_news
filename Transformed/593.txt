
2001 - Measles in the Republic of Korea

09 February 2001 Disease Outbreak Reported

A total of  39 537 cases including six deaths
has been reported nationwide from March 2000 through January 2001.  The outbreak
originally started in the eastern part of the country with steady acceleration in the
number of cases since October 2000.  7449 cases have been reported in January 2001.
The outbreak has been laboratory confirmed serologically and by isolation of measles virus
from nine cases.

The Ministry of Health of the Republic of Korea is carrying
out active surveillance, education and case management and stricter school entry
requirements are in place. A school-based vaccination campaign is being prepared for May
2001.
