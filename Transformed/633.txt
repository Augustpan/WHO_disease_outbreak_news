
2002 - Cholera in Malawi

13 September 2002 Disease Outbreak Reported

Following a WHO mission to Malawi, the reporting of cholera cases has been revised.
As of  26 August 2002, a total of  773 cases and 41 deaths  (case-fatality
rate,  5.13%) were reported in the Nkhotakota
area since the beginning of the year.  From 
5 May to 26 August, 339 cases and 25 deaths  (case-fatality rate, 7.37%) were reported in the
same area.

WHO, along with UNICEF is assisting the Ministry of Health in providing on-site training in case
management and cholera control.  UNICEF is providing
additional supplies.
