
2000 - Meningococcal disease in Saudi Arabia and the Netherlands - Update

27 April 2000 Disease Outbreak Reported

Saudi Arabia-Update: Health authorities
have reported a revised total of 225 cases, including 57 deaths since the Haj last month.
  Bacterial investigations have now confirmed Neisseria meningitidis
serogroup A in 54 cases, serogroup W135 in 50 cases, and serogroup B in 1 case.  The
situation is being closely monitored by the health authorities, with case management and
contact tracing in place.

Netherlands-Update: There have been 2
confirmed cases of N. meningitidis serogroup W135 and 2 probable cases, including
1 death reported.  Case management and contact tracing are also being carried out.

Please see the previous report
of meningococcal disease in these two countries.
