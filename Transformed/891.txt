
Suspected acute haemorrhagic fever syndrome in the Republic of the Congo - Update

10 February 2003
Disease Outbreak Reported


An assessment team has today left WHO headquarters  to support the investigation of rumours of cases of acute haemorrhagic fever syndrome in Mbomo district, Cuvette Ouest region. The team will be working with the WHO Country Office and the WHO Regional Office for Africa in support of the outbreak response.
