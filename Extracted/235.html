1998 - Meningococcal Meningitis in Angola

06 August 1998

Disease Outbreak Reported

The Ministry of Health has reported a total of 574 cases of meningococcal meningitis (group A) from 1 January to 27 July 1998 of which 69 have died (case fatality ratio 12%).
