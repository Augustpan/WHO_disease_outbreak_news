1996 - Dengue/dengue haemorrhagic fever in India - Update 2

15 November 1996

Disease Outbreak Reported

The daily number of new cases reported by the National Institute of Communicable Diseases in Delhi has remained below 100 in the last few days (92 on 4 November, 83 on 5 November, 84 on 6 November and 85 on 7 November. The total number of cases reported up to 7 November was 8 514 of which 336 (3.9%) were fatal.
