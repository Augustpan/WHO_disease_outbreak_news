Poliomyelitis in Indonesia - update 2

9 June 2005

On 9 June 2005, six new polio cases were confirmed in Indonesia, bringing the total number of cases to 34. Five of these cases are from neighbouring districts as the index case (Lebak, Cianjur and Bogor districts), while one of these new cases is from the same district as the index case (Sukabumi district).
