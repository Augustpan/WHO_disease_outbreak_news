1997 - Plague in Mozambique

08 August 1997

Disease Outbreak Reported

The Ministry of Health has reported that 115 cases of plague have occurred between 7 June and 4 July in Mutarara District, Tete Province, a plague endemic zone. No deaths have been reported.
