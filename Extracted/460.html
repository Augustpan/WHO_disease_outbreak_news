2000 - Meningococcal disease in the United States

26 April 2000

Disease Outbreak Reported

As of 20 April, 3 confirmed cases of serogroup W135 meningococcal disease have been reported by the New York City Communicable Disease Program. One case is a returning pilgrim with as yet unconfirmed vaccination status, and 1 is a household contact of a returning pilgrim. The third patient did not participate in the Haj and is not known to have close contacts among pilgrims who traveled to Mecca. In the United States, W135 accounts for only 3%-4% of sporadic cases of meningococcal disease.
