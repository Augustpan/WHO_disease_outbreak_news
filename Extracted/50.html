1996 - Outbreak of food-borne intoxication in India

12 August 1996

Disease Outbreak Reported

As at 9 August, 105 patients had been hospitalized in Bombay with food-borne intoxication. Experts from the National Institute for Communicable Diseases, Delhi, have been called to investigate the cause of outbreak and to advise on its control. WHO Regional Office for South East Asia and Headquarters facilitated delivery of antitoxin.
