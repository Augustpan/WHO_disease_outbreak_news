2000 - Cholera in South Africa - Update

18 October 2000

Disease Outbreak Reported

The Kwazulu-Natal Department of Health has reported 3 075 cases and 26 deaths since the start of the outbreak in mid-August 2000 (see previous report).

The outbreak is in Kwazulu-Natal, affecting the following areas: Lower Umfolozi Districts which include Ngwelezane and Empangeni, Eshowe/Nkandla areas, Stanger area and Port Shepstone area.
