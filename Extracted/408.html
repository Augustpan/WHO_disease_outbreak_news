2000 - Ebola haemorrhagic fever in Uganda - Update 11

27 October 2000

Disease Outbreak Reported

As of 27 October, the Ugandan Ministry of Health has reported 191 cases, including 68 deaths.

As described in an earlier report, the team in Gulu is carrying out its work through these major areas of activity: coordination and logistics; case management; epidemiological surveillance; public education; laboratory; and media relations.  Several organizations are participating in these activities in Gulu district and at national level through the National Task Force for the Control of Viral Haemorrhagic Fevers:

  • Ugandan Local Government Officials
