Poliomyelitis in Indonesia - update 3

14 June 2005

On 14 June 2005, five new polio cases were confirmed in Indonesia, bringing the total number of cases to 39. Three of these cases are from the same district as the index case (Sukabumi district), and two are from a neighbouring district (Bogor district).
